h - to move left
j - to move up
k - to move down
g - to move right

i - activate insert mode
I - activate insert mode (on start line)

a - activate insert mode
A - activate insert mode (on end line)

ctrl+c or esc - back no normal mode

e - to jump on words
gg - to jump on the start
GG - to jump on the end

number+gg - jump to the line

o - create new line
dd - delete this hole line
